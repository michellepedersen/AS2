ABOUT US

The Haukai Restaurant is located in the Bay of Islands. We specilise in Maori Kai. The Haukai Restaurant brings you Maori culture and traditions in everything we do. Our Haukai team would like to invite you to a true New Zealand experience from the moment you step into the restaurant. Our menu is showcasing the traditional food of our local people.

ABOUT THE OWNERS

The owners work in the business full-time. They are keen on creating a business that appeals to locals and tourists visiting the area. They both come from a Maori background and have lived in the area all their lives. They are welcoming of everyone to their restaurant and enjoy sharing their culture. Come say hi we would love to meet you.
